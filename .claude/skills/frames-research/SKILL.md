# FRAMES Research

Use this skill for cited decisions about analogy, metaphor, cognition,
communication, dashboards, traffic conventions, or safety framing.

## Protocol

1. State the research question.
2. Prefer primary or high-quality sources.
3. Separate evidence from design interpretation.
4. Record implications for the frame schema or catalog.
5. Add limitations and open questions.

## Output Shape

Research notes should include:

- Question.
- Sources.
- Findings.
- Design implications.
- Limits.
- Follow-up work.
