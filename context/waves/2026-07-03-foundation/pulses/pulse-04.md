# Pulse 04: Fit rubric

## Goal

Create a simple rubric for choosing, rejecting, and revising frames.

## Candidate Checks

- Does the frame produce a concrete action cue?
- Does it clarify priority, timing, risk, or responsibility?
- Does it preserve human dignity?
- Does it hide evidence, ownership, or uncertainty?
- Does it travel across the expected audience?
