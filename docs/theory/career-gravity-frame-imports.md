boundaries, failure modes, and promotion criteria.

patterns can be practically useful while still remaining local observations
until FRAMES scores and role-reviews them for broader use.

|---|---|
| Source scene | A gate with multiple locks where one locked point blocks passage. |
| Target situation | A decision or readiness model with several required dimensions where one critical blocker dominates averages. |
| Frame job | Risk / priority. |
| Story job | Decision / tradeoff or warning. |
| Claim strength | `locally_observed`. |

## Cross-Import Lessons

- CAREER Gravity contributes strong risk, priority, transfer, and momentum
  frames, but employment-sensitive uses need plain-language fallback.
- `Veto Rule`, `Reset Tax`, and `Momentum State` are likely reusable outside
  career contexts after role review.
- `Mentor vs. Sponsor` and `Advocacy Bandwidth` need stronger human-safety
  warnings because they touch power, reputation, and fairness.
- Future crate metadata should support `claim_strength`, `application_pack`,
  `draft_status`, and transfer fields before adding career-derived entries to
  the public starter catalog.
